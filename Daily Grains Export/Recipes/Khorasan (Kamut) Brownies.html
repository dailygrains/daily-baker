Khorasan (Kamut) Brownies

Daily Grains

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Source: breadtopia.com

Ingredients

10 Tbsp unsalted butter (142 grams)

2 1/4 cups sugar (450 grams)

2/3 cups oil e.g. avocado oil (130 grams)

4 eggs + 2 yolks

2 tsp vanilla extract (8 grams)

1 1/2 cups cocoa powder (150 grams)

1 cup khorasan whole grain flour (140 grams)

2 Tbsp cornstarch (14 grams)

1/2 tsp salt (3 grams)

1/4 tsp baking soda (1-2 grams)

1 1/2 cups dark or semi-sweet chocolate chips (250 grams)

optional 1 1/2 cups toasted walnuts or pecans (175 grams)

Directions

Preheat your oven to 325Â°F and line a 9" x 13" pan with lightly oiled parchment paper. Halve the ingredients if you prefer to use an 8" x 8" pan. The cook time is for a metal pan. Glass will take longer.

If you plan to use nuts, toast them in a pan for a few minutes until they're aromatic and more golden, then set aside.

Melt the butter and sugar together in the microwave or on the stove.

Mix in the oil, eggs and yolks, and vanilla extract.

Add the cocoa powder and stir until there are almost no lumps.

In another bowl, briefly whisk together the flour, cornstarch, salt, and baking soda.

Mix in these dry ingredients.

Fold in the chocolate chips and nuts, if desired.

Scrape the batter into your pan and spread it with a spatula.

Bake for 30 minutes. The corners will be a little toasted and the center soft but it will set while cooling.

If you can resist the temptation to eat them hot, allowing the brownies to cool before removing them from the pan and cutting them. This will make them more sturdy and deepen the flavors.

Store in a closed container for 3-5 days.
