RMSA Rosemary Kitchen Trials Sourdough

Bread, Daily Grains

Ingredients

Moved from 40% to 34% kamut…updated levain, bread flour, salt in March 2025

385g bread flour

187g Kamut flour

114.4g levain

11.4g salt (not iodized)

22g olive oil

390g water

5.5g fresh rosemary

TOTAL: 1083g

Directions

1. Build Levain the night before, letting it set covered at room temperature for 6-10 hours - Mix 50g Bread Flour, 50g water, and 1 Tbsp Starter

2. Mill whole wheat flours to desired texture

3. Mix flours (250g Bread Flour and 250g Whole Wheat Flour); hand mix flour with 360g water and 100g Levain

- Autolyze for 30-45 mins (Autolyzation is a resting period that allows flour to absorb moisture. Enzymes are activated and the gluten proteins, gliadin and glutenin, are assimilated into the dough)

4. Incorporate <15g water and 10g salt into dough

5. Stretch and fold to strengthen dough and develop gluten network

- Stretch and fold 3-6 times in 30 minute increments

6. Preshape dough on floured surface and let Bench Rest for 30 mins
- Preshaping is done by folding the edges of the dough into the center to a circular

shape. Flip the seam side down an cover with a towel or plastic to Bench Rest

7. Final Shape and place bread in banneton or loaf pan with parchment paper
- Proof bread for 1-3 hours (Bread will be proofed when the microbes in your dough have

fully leavened the loaf. Poke the dough with finger, if the impression slowly bounces back, your bread is proved and ready to bake)

8. Score the top of the loaf with razor, lame or scissors to allow for steam release

9. If using a Dutch Oven, Preheat Dutch Oven for 20 minutes at 500°F

- If using a loaf pan, preheat oven to 450°F

10. Place bread on parchment paper that has been lightly dusted with cornmeal or flour

11. Turn heat down to 450°F and bake for 20 minutes with Dutch Oven lid on
- If using a loaf pan, put a pan of water in the oven to create steam and encourage crust

development. Bake loaf for 32-40 mins, taking water pan out at 20 minutes

- Take Dutch oven lid off at 20 minutes and bake for 15-18 minutes longer

12. Let bread cool to room temperature before slicing and serving
